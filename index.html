Why choose Extendra Hub instead of traditional video calls?

Extendra Hub offers advanced virtual spaces that enhance communication and collaboration beyond traditional video calls. Their platform boosts engagement and productivity in meetings, training sessions, and collaborative work.

Enhanced presence and real-time interaction

Extendra Hub provides an embodied presence, offering physical and mental immersion in virtual spaces. Unlike video calls that can feel disconnected, this platform enables real-time interactions, making users feel as if they are in the same room.

Unmatched hyperrealism and scalability

Extendra Hub sets itself apart with meticulous attention to detail. Their virtual spaces use materials, textures, and lighting that mimic real-world conditions for an authentic experience. The 3D objects are accurately scaled, which is crucial for industries requiring precise measurements and representations.
